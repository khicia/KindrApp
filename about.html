About Kindr

The Kindr Application Project was created to provide an accessible platform for helping communities affected by disasters in the Philippines. It aims to make donating easier, faster, and more transparent, ensuring that aid reaches those who need it most, while also serving as a practical application of our knowledge in system integration and architecture.
