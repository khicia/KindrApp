BAGYONG TINO UPDATE

MANILA, Philippines — Typhoon Tino, internationally known as Kalmaegi, has left 204 people dead, 109 others missing, and 156 others injured, the Office of Civil Defense said on Saturday. Due to the effects of Tino, which has made eight landfalls before exiting on Thursday, President Ferdinand Marcos Jr. declared a state of national calamity.
